# Code of Conduct

## Our commitment

We are committed to making participation in Agent Trace Workbench a respectful, harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of background, identity, ability, experience, or point of view.

## Expected behavior

- Be respectful, constructive, and specific.
- Assume good intent while addressing technical and safety concerns directly.
- Respect privacy. Never post another person's prompts, responses, tool output, credentials, local paths, or Agent history.
- Accept maintainers' decisions about project scope, local-first boundaries, and responsible disclosure.

## Unacceptable behavior

- Harassment, discrimination, threats, sexualized attention, or personal attacks.
- Publishing private information without explicit permission.
- Uploading secrets or real captured Agent data to issues, pull requests, or discussions.
- Disruptive conduct that makes collaboration unsafe or unproductive.

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may edit, remove, or reject contributions and may temporarily or permanently restrict participation when behavior violates this code. Reports should be made privately through the repository's security or maintainer contact channel. Reports will be reviewed fairly and kept confidential to the extent possible.

## Scope

This code applies in project spaces and when someone publicly represents the project or its community.
